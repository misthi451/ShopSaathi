Help & FAQ
Common Questions
Is ShopSaathi free for shopkeepers? ▾
Yes, 100% free. There are no listing fees, no commissions, and no monthly charges. ShopSaathi is completely free for all shopkeepers across India.
How long does it take for my shop to appear in search? ▾
After you register, our team verifies your shop within 24 hours. Once verified, your shop will appear in search results and you'll receive an SMS confirmation.
How can I update my product list or prices? ▾
After your shop is verified, you'll receive login credentials via SMS. You can log in to your shopkeeper dashboard to update products, prices, and stock availability anytime.
Can customers from my village or small town find my shop? ▾
Yes! ShopSaathi covers cities, towns, and villages across all of India. If your location isn't listed, we'll add it when you register your shop.
How do I report incorrect shop information? ▾
Every shop card has a "Report" button. Click it and select the issue — wrong address, closed permanently, wrong phone number, etc. Our team reviews reports within 24 hours.
How do I install ShopSaathi as an app on my phone? ▾
Click the "Install App" button in the top navigation bar (on supported browsers), or use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" option. ShopSaathi works offline once installed.
Is my phone number shared with everyone? ▾
Your registered phone number is shown on your shop's public listing so customers can contact you. Customer phone numbers are never stored or shared. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
